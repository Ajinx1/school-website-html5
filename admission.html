Application options for applicants applying within Ghana

Option A

1. Download the Printable Version of the Application Form. Print and Complete it.

2. Go to any branch of Ghana Commercial Bank in the country and Pay-in Your Application Processing Fee (Our Bank details and the Fees are on the Application Form).

3.Send the Completed Form and a photocopy of the pay-in (cash deposit) slip to us by post or by registered mail.

Please Download the AIT Printable Application form

Option B

1.Access the Fillable Version of the Application Form

2. Complete it on the Computer without first printing it

3. Send the completed form to us as an e-mail attachment to the e-mail address on the form

4. Go to any Ghana Commercial Bank Branch and Pay-in Your application Processing

5. Fee (Our Bank details and the Fees are on the Application Form)

6. Write your full name on top of the pay-in slip

7. Either scan and send the pay-in (cash deposit) slip to us as an e-mail attachment or send a photocopy of the pay-in slip to us by post or by registered mail.

Please Access and Complete the AIT fillable Application form

Options for applicants applying from outside Ghana

Option A

1. Download the Printable Version of the Application Form. Print and Complete it.

2. Go to any branch of Ecobank in your country and Pay-in Your Application Processing Fee (Our Bank details and the Fees are on the Application Form).

3.Send the Completed Form and a photocopy of the pay-in (cash deposit) slip to us by post or by registered mail.

Please Click here to Download the of the Printable Version Application Form

Option B

1.Access the Fillable Version of the Application Form

2. Complete it on the Computer without first printing it

3. Send the completed form to us as an e-mail attachment to the e-mail address on the form

4. Follow the Instructions on the Form on how to pay your Application Processing Fee
