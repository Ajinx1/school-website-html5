MITRABURG UNIVERSITY APPLICATION INFORMATION

This page contains all the information you need to know when it comes to applying for one of our undergraduate or graduate taught degree programmes .  Information on when to apply and entry requirements can also be found here .

WHEN TO APPLY


There is no specific deadline to apply for most courses, and applications will be considered until the course is full. However , to allow time for us to process your application we recommend that you apply by the following dates for admission in October : You should apply for a undergraduate or graduate taught course - Bsc, MSc, etc . - as early as possible . We recommend that UK students should apply by 1 August for entry in October .
International students should apply by 1 June or earlier, if possible , to ensure there is enough time to get a visa , if applicable.
In order to be successful in your application for one of our Programs be it graduate or undergraduate, you will typically need to meet the criteria that was stated on our Admission page Here .


HOW TO APPLY FOR ANY OF OUR PROGRAMS

The quickest and easiest way to apply for a Program at the MitraBurg university is through our online application service . This allows you to complete your application information , attach electronic copies of your academic transcripts , certificates and other supporting information and also provides a facility for an email request to be sent to your referees enabling them to send your supporting references directly to us.

WHAT HAPPENS AFTER YOU APPLY

The University has a simple process for assessing your application as outlined below :

ACKNOWLEDGEMENT

When we receive your application form , we will send you an acknowledgement of receipt.

DECISION

Your application will then be passed on to the Admissions Tutor for your degree course . The Admissions Tutor will decide whether to offer you a place . To ensure that a decision is made as quickly as possible , please ensure that you include all the necessary documentation , including references , with your application form .

Successful Applications

If your application is successful , we will send you a letter offering you a place at the University and explaining any conditions that you have to meet . We will also include accommodation information if you will be studying at the University full - time.

Unsuccessful Applications

If we decide that we are unable to offer you a place , you will receive a letter from us letting you know this .

STATUS OF APPLICATION If you have any questions regarding the status of your application , you should us through our contact page Here.
